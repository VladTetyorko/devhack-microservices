About DevHack

DevHack is an interview preparation tool designed to help developers organize and manage their interview preparation process. Whether you're preparing for technical interviews at top tech companies or just want to keep your skills sharp, DevHack provides the tools you need.

With DevHack, you can:

  • Browse and manage interview questions across different difficulty levels
  • Record your answers to track your progress over time
  • Take notes on specific questions or topics
  • Organize content with tags for easy filtering
  • Collaborate with other users to share knowledge

How to Use

Getting started with DevHack is easy:

  1. Browse the Questions section to find interview questions
  2. Filter questions by difficulty or Tags to focus on specific areas
  3. Record your Answers to track your progress
  4. Create Notes to document your learning
  5. Manage your User Profile to personalize your experience
